# Project Charter

## Engagement
- **Client**: Undivided Ventures

## Problem Statement
Undivided Ventures needed a practical way to evaluate whether ESG-focused upgrades in real estate would generate attractive financial returns while improving sustainability outcomes.

## Objectives
1. Build a financial model that quantifies ESG investment value.
2. Benchmark sustainable real estate projects versus peers.
3. Produce five-year projections and scenario-based P&L outcomes.
4. Provide Excel-based visuals suitable for internal decision making and client presentations.

## Scope
### In Scope
- Capital expenditure and operating expenditure impact modeling
- Utility savings and rental premium estimates
- Occupancy improvements from sustainability positioning
- Incentive/subsidy incorporation
- Payback and IRR-style investment logic
- Peer benchmarking and scenario comparisons

### Out of Scope
- Full legal due diligence
- Detailed engineering feasibility studies
- Market-by-market permit modeling

## Key Questions Answered
- What is the expected payback period of ESG retrofit packages?
- How sensitive value creation is to energy prices, occupancy, and rent uplift?
- How does the target project compare to sustainable peers?
- What downside risks could delay value realization?

## Deliverables
- Assumption library and model framework
- Peer benchmark comparison table
- Five-year operating projection by scenario
- Scenario P&L table (Downside/Base/Upside)
- Excel-ready chart dataset and presentation narrative
